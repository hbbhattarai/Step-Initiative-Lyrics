पागल जस्तै छु म त Pagal Jastai Chhu Ma Ta

✧ ४ भजन · 4 Stanzas Final ✧
१
Pagal jastai chhu ma ta,
Pagal jastai chhu.
Pagal jastai chhu ma ta,
Pagal jastai chhu.
Chinne lai Ram Bhakta,
Nachinne lai pagal.
Chinne lai Ram Bhakta,
Nachinne lai pagal jasti chu.
२
Na bhole ni bhanchan sabai le,
Bol dai nayo pagal jastai.
Bolau hudai hira pani,
Boldai hincha pagal jastai.
Rama japau deu sabai le,
Sheyma jape deu,
Muk lay malai pagal bhana,
Man lay payro rama jape deu.
३
Haase pani bhanchhan sabai le,
Haastai hidchha pagal jastai.
Royapani sukha china,
Royapani bhanchhan pagal jastai.
Ram ko nama lay,
Aunai sheyam ko namalay,
Pagal jastai bhanayo,
Aja malai samko namalay.
४
Garna bhanchan garchora,
Duldai hinacha pagal jastai.
Jha gayo thaya bhanchan,
Ayo auta pagal jastai.
China sakina ni mailay,
Ramlai China sakina.
Duniya ko chalamala,
Launa maila bujna shakina.

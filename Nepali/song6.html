श्रीत कमला स्तोत्रम् Sritha Kamala Stotram · जयदेव अष्टपदी
🎤 जयदेव · Jayadeva
✧ translation by P.R.Ramachander · Raja Thatha ✧
१
Sritha kamala kucha mandala,
Dhritha kundala hey,
Kalitha vana mala,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
You rest on the lotus like breasts of Goddess Lakshmi, Oh god who wears swinging ear globes, and also wear garland of forest flowers, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
२
Dina mani mandala mandana,
Bhava khandana hey,
Muni jana manasa hamsa,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Your face shines like the disc of Sun, Oh destroyer of the ties of birth and death, Oh swan in the hearts of great sages, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
३
Kaliya visha dhara ganjana,
Jana ranjana hey,
Yadu kula nalina dinesa,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Yadu kula nalina dinesa,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
You who defeated the poison bearing Kaliya, Oh God who entertains lots of people, and who is the lotus of the clan of Yadus, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
४
Madhu Mura naraka vinasana,
Garudasana hey,
Sura kula kelee nidhana,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
You who killed Madhu, Mura and Narakasura, Oh God who sits on Garuda, and who entertains devas by his sports, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
५
Amala kamala dala lochana,
Bhava mochana hey,
Tribhuvana bhuvana nidhana,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
You are having holy lotus flower like eyes, Oh God who frees us from ties of birth, and whose home is all the three worlds, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
६
Janaka suthaa krutha bhooshana,
Jitha dhooshana hey,
Samara samitha dasa kanta,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
You who made Goddess Sita wear ornaments, Oh defeater of bad ones, who in the war killed the ten headed Ravana, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
७
Abhinava jala dhara sundara,
Dhrutha mandara hey,
Sri mukha chandra chakora,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
You who are as pretty as the rich new cloud, Oh God who lifted the mountain, and whose face is like the moon loved by Chakora birds, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
८
Thava charanam pranatha vayam,
Ithi bhavaya hey,
Kuru kusalam pranatheshu,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
We salute at your feet, Oh God who gives prosperity, please look after the good of all beings, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.
९
Sri Jayadeva kaver idham,
Krutha mudam hey,
Mangala ujjwala githam,
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Jaya jaya deva hare.
Oh darling God, this shining and auspicious song, written by poet Jayadeva, is being offered to you, Victory to God Jaya deva and to Lord Hari.

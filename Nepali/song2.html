Song Meaning

This beautiful devotional song expresses a deep personal connection with Lord Krishna. The title translates to "In my heart is a photo of Krishna." The lyrics reflect on the transient nature of life (jiwan hamro choto chha - "our life is short") and the dualities of joy and sorrow, wealth and poverty. Through keeping Krishna in one's heart, the singer finds the spiritual perspective to navigate life's ups and downs and ultimately find contentment (sontosh).

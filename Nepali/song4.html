मिलिजुली गाउँ अरती हरिको Milijuli Ghauhn Arrati Hari Ko
❀ first stanza
Milijuli ghauhn arrati Hari ko,
Milijuli ghauhn arrati.
Deen dayala mero man basiyo,
Timi bina ko arrati.
(Milijuli ghauhn arrati Hari ko...)
❀ second stanza
Gyanako deep prajwalit garauhn,
Prem sugandhit dhoop.
Bhaaw bhakti le sajauhn thali,
Hridaya kamal ko roop.
(Milijuli ghauhn arrati Hari ko...)
❀ third stanza
Shankha ghanta ra mridanga baja,
Baje koili tara.
Dev dev Hari aajai aunu,
Darshan dinu hamra.
(Milijuli ghauhn arrati Hari ko...)
❀ fourth stanza
Chandan chola laal chadhauhnla,
Phool mala shringar.
Arrati gaey harshit bhaey,
Bho yo bhakti ko bhandar.
(Milijuli ghauhn arrati Hari ko...)
❀ fifth stanza
Shata shata naman chhati bhari pranam,
Yahi ho vinay hamiro.
Rakhyo jo jodi charan timro,
Rinai chha rina timro.
(Milijuli ghauhn arrati Hari ko...)
